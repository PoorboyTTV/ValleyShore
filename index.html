Frequently Asked Questions

No experience needed for flat-water rentals on Osoyoos Lake. We give you a quick orientation at delivery — how to paddle, how to fall safely, and how to wear your life jacket. If you're completely new, a tandem kayak or single kayak is a great starting point.
Every rental includes paddles, life jackets (PFDs) in all sizes, and dry bags. We also offer waterproof phone pouches and water shoes on request. Delivery and pickup to your chosen location are always free within our service area.
We drive the equipment directly to your launch spot — a beach, dock, Airbnb, campground, wherever you're launching from. We set everything up, walk you through the gear, and then come back to pick it all up when your rental time ends. No truck or trailer required on your end.
We understand plans change. Cancellations made 48+ hours before your rental start time receive a full refund or credit. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee depending on circumstances. We're a small local business, so we always try to work something out — just call or text us.
Safety is our top priority. If there are dangerous wind or weather conditions on your booking day, we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge. Osoyoos typically has fantastic summer weather, but we always monitor forecasts. Minor clouds or light wind are generally fine for paddling.
Yes! Use the booking form and select "2 Days", "3 Days", or "Full Week" from the duration dropdown. Multi-day rentals get our best per-day rate. For week-long rentals or special group arrangements, give us a call at 250-320-7592 for a custom quote.
Children are welcome! Renters must be 18+ to sign for the equipment. Children must be supervised by an adult and wear a properly fitted life jacket at all times. We carry child-sized PFDs. Tandem kayaks are great for parents paddling with kids.
Payment is collected at delivery. We accept tap/NFC, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash, and e-Transfer. Have a promo code? Enter it in the booking form before submitting to apply your discount automatically.
